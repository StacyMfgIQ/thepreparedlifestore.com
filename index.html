The 6-tool suite

Every tool addresses a real moment in the family's journey.

Each one is fully interactive, mobile-optimized, and white-labeled with your facility name, logo, brand color, and a custom call-to-action.

⭐ Highest engagement
Tool 01
Home Safety Walkthrough
Room-by-room guided assessment that identifies fall risks, safety gaps, and recommended modifications. Families use this before they're ready for anything else — it meets them exactly where they are.
→ CTA: "Talk to our care team about next steps"
Tool 02
Care Options Navigator
Interactive assessment that walks a family through their loved one's current health, safety, and independence — and surfaces the care levels most appropriate for their situation, with plain-language explanations of each option.
→ CTA: "Schedule a tour of our community"
Tool 03
Elder Care Cost Estimator
Personalized monthly cost estimate with a 3-year projection, coverage gap analysis, and regional cost data. Answers the question every family is afraid to ask — and positions your facility as the honest resource they can trust.
→ CTA: "Request a personalized cost conversation"
Tool 04
Family Meeting Facilitator
Structured guide for the hardest conversation most families will ever have. Includes pre-written discussion starters, a commitment record, and a printable summary — so everyone leaves on the same page.
→ CTA: "Our counselors can help guide your family"
Tool 05
Caregiver Burnout Check-In
Scored 8-question burnout assessment with wellbeing insights and resources. Reaches the exhausted caregiver — the person most likely to make a placement decision — at exactly the moment they need to feel seen.
→ CTA: "Let us help carry some of this with you"
Tool 06
Care Options Comparison Workbook
Side-by-side comparison of care settings — costs, services, what to look for, and questions to ask. Helps families evaluate options clearly and objectively, with your community positioned as the natural next step.
→ CTA: "Compare us — we welcome the conversation"
